Description

The Vampiric Council is the longest running organization/council ever, with over 500 sub-councils worldwide and experiencing gradual growth. We exist to discuss and make decisions regarding the issues that vampires face. Our goal is the expansion of our species as well as to rule all other species as we are a body of vampires hell-bent on conquering the world. We are a diverse group of people. We do not discriminate unless you are a werewolf or a vampire hunter.
